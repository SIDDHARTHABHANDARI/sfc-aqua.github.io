· Odakyu Enoshima Line/Sotetsu IzuMino Line/Yokohama Subway Blue Line Get off at "湘南台 Shonandai" station. From West Exit of Shonandai, ride KanaChuBus 湘24(sho-24) line bound for "慶應大学 Keio University". After 15 minutes, get off at "慶應大学本館前　 Keio daigaku honkanmae".

・JR Tokaido Line Get off at "辻堂 Tsujido".From North Exit of Tsujido, ride Kanachu Bus 辻34(Tsuji-34)line bound for "慶應大学 Keio University".After 25 minutes, get off at "慶應大学本館前　 Keio daigaku honkanmae".
