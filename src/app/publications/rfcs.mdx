RFCs are peer-reviewed, usually collaboratively-written, open documents that represent the shared understanding of the community on a given topic, ultimately leading to the standards that govern the behavior of nodes and applications on the Internet. If you have questions about RFCs as scholarly publications, see [this article](https://www.sigcomm.org/node/2819) by Brian E. Carpenter and Craig Partridge.
